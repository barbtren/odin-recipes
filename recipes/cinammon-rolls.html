Fluffy Cinammon Rolls

Description

When I think about cozy, comforting foods, one of the first things that comes to mind is cinammon rolls. There's nothing quite like tucking into a fluffy, warm cinammon roll straight out of the oven on a cold and rainy day.

I have gone through several iterations of this recipe in the last few years on my hunt for the fluffiest, softest and most flavourful cinammon rolls. This recipe pours cream over the rolls before baking, infusing them with a rich and soft quality that can't be beat!

Ingredients

Dough Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/2 tsp instant yeast
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • pinch of cardamom
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup of cream/coconut milk/evaporated milk note: do not add this to the dough

Filling Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp cinammon
  • pinch of salt (omit if butter is salted)

Steps

  1. Mix warm milk and yeast, let sit for 5 minutes until frothy
  2. Add eggs, butter and sugar, and mix until combined
  3. Add salt, cardamom, and 4 cups flour
  4. Mix by hand or in stand mixer until barely combined, then allow to rest for 5 minutes so flour can absorb liquid
  5. Knead by hand or with dough hook for 5-7 minutes or until dough is smooth. The dough should be soft and tacky, do not add too much flour or buns will be tough
  6. Cover dough and rest for 10-30 minutes
  7. Mix up the filling
  8. Sprinkle flour on clean work surface and roll out the dough to a rectangle approximately 24"x15"
  9. Spread filling evenly over the dough, roll up and slice into 12-18 rolls using a serrated knife
  10. Place rolls into a prepared baking dish, cover and allow to rise for 20-40 minutes
  11. While rolls are rising, preheat oven to 375° farenheit
  12. Before placing rolls in oven, warm up 1/2 cup of cream slightly and pour over the risen rolls
  13. Bake in preheated oven for 20-30 minutes, do not overbake
  14. Allow to cool slightly before topping with icing of your choice if desired
